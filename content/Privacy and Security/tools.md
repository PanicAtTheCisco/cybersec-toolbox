## Privacy and Security Tools

### Tools
* [Digital Defense Checklist](https://digital-defense.io/)
    * Gauge your Digital Defense coverage visually (not exhaustive list, just helpful)
* [PrivacySpy](https://privacyspy.org/)
    * Crowdsourced TLDRs of Privacy Policies
* [Terms of Service Didn't Read](https://tosdr.org/en)
    * Crowdsourced TLDRs of companies Terms of Service
* [The GNU Privacy Guard](https://gnupg.org/documentation/howtos.html)
    * FOSS PGP encryption tool
* [TOR Project](https://www.torproject.org/)
    * Access the TOR network
* [Firefox Hardening](https://github.com/arkenfox/user.js)
    * Arkenfox user.js (Gold standard for Firefox Hardening)
* [Fake Name Generator](https://www.fakenamegenerator.com/)
    * Helpful for generating pseudonyms
* [Bug Me Not](https://bugmenot.com/)
    * Account sharing platform
* [VPN Relation Map](https://windscribe.com/vpnmap)
    * How different VPNs are related to each other
* [NoMoreRansom](https://www.nomoreransom.org/en/decryption-tools.html)
    * An initiative by a number of law enforcement agencies to provide free decryption tools to a variety of ransomware to help victims get their data back without paying
